The Complete Package For Gardening & Agricultural Websites
Gardenko is an Iconic WordPress Theme that is specially built for Gardening, Landscaping, Lawn Services, Groundskeeping, Florists, Agriculture related businesses websites.
Gardenko is the perfect match for Several Areas Like

Business & Services:Agricultural, Landscaping, Farming, Florists, Lawn & Garden Installation, Water management, Flooring, Pool installation, Weed management & more
Blog: Suitable for the all kind of gardening, environmental, NGOs blogs  articles websites

We have built Gardenko so easy & very compatible so that you can use it for create any of your needs businesses websites. Theme includes all the important inner pages such as About Us, Your Services, Projects, Gallery, Contact Us, Team, News, Reviews etc. The theme is very easy to customize with Elementor page builder and it has a lot of features and very strong admin panel for any client to make a good website quickly.
Looking for release your website ? This theme is best choice.
