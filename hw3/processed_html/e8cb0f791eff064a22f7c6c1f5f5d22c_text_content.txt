SEO Area Importance vs Website Size: Some time ago the awesome Mark Williams-Cook shared a brilliantly simple but easy to understand chart as one of his unsolicited SEO tips (which you can see here: https://lnkd.in/dsdFWfTJ) where he showed how the biggest the site, the most important technical issues/opportunities tend to be, and how content is more important for smaller sites. 

I've used this graph as a base to integrate another critical area: backlinks; as well as to add nuance to the content aspect: 

1. Add another important area of SEO work: Backlinks. Which also (in most cases, although there might be exceptions) become less of an issue the biggest the size of the site, as they tend to be more authoritative and established, with more content attracting backlinks naturally too. 

2. I've also added a bit of "nuance" to the content line: The relative importance/value of content will tend to decrease the biggest the site is, however, it won't lose its value so quickly, it will hold its importance up to a point, even from a "maintenance" standpoint to keep info up to date, show accurate info, and give good user experience beyond search, so I've changed the chart to reflect this. 

I hope you like it and find useful to show the value of different areas to clients/stakeholders üëá

The chart is kinda silly. Why not just say "Its more likely that big sites can benefit from technical SEO"?

My "Tip": Measure your sites vs competition and then decide what is important. 

Deciding what is important for a site simply based on its size is actually ridiculous. While it is more LIKELY that technical will have bigger impact for very large sites, all the conclusions from the chart do not follow. 

Sites don't live in a vacuum, and every signal is relative, especially in the zero sum game of ranking. 

All big sites don't just have better link graphs than their competitors. Even if they did, why would links become less important?

What about the sophistication of the operation? What if they are already strong technically? How does technical become more important as the site get larger? 

Content may be the best way to expand from a solid foundation. Why does content keep getting less important?
