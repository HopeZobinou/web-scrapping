Online Documentation

Erlinews is a highly customizable and feature-rich platform built with Elementor, a popular drag-and-drop page builder that enables you to create stunning and responsive designs without any coding knowledge.

In addition to the core Elementor features, this theme comes with an Elementor add-on that further expands the capabilities of the page builder. It also includes a One Click Demo Importer that allows you to quickly and easily set up your website with pre-designed templates and layouts.

With 7 header styles and 6 footer styles, this theme offers a wide range of customization options for your website’s layout. Additionally, it includes 6 single post layouts with different post format options, 5 category templates, and 5 tag templates, giving you flexibility in the way you display your content.

Erlinews also includes a child theme, making it easy for you to customize and modify the theme without affecting the core code. The Redux Admin Panel provides you with a user-friendly interface for managing your theme’s settings and options.

You’ll have access to custom widgets, author post, post lists, and more, that allow you to add additional functionality and style to your website. The theme supports unlimited Google Fonts, ensuring that your website’s typography is both beautiful and legible.

Erlinews is cross-browser compatible, meaning it will work seamlessly on all major web browsers. And finally, detailed documentation is included to help you navigate and use all of the theme’s features and options.
