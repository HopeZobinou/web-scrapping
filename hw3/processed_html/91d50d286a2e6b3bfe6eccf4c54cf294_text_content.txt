"Austin Powers" actress Heather Graham is spicing up her Instagram timeline after a romantic Italian trip with snowboarder John de Neufville.    Graham, 53, took to Instagram on Sunday to share some snaps from their trip and included a picture of herself wading in the water in a black bikini."Italian Getaway," she captioned the post with a few Italian-inspired emojis. Graham concluded with "#amalficoast."   Heather Graham and John de Neufville enjoy a romantic Italian vacation.
