Satka - Satellite TV & Internet Provider WordPress Theme

 

Satka is a modern WordPress theme exclusively designed for broadband TV and Internet provider service, satellite TV and online video streaming service, fits for a crypto startup website and for a modern IT company website. It’s trendy, fully responsive and easy to customize. Satka is a great solution for any Telecom or IT company that strives to stand out among millions of sites and convey its vision to the client. Satka gives the ability to create a precise-looking and creative website in short time.

We are very excited to provide an excellent product with awesome updates for our customers! If you have any questions or want to suggest an improvement please visit our Help Center and Theme Documentation.

The theme is fully compatible with easy to use drag and drop Elementor visual builder, WooCommerce and translation plugins like WPML and TranslatePress.
