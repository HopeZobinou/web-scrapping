Viral Video Shows Israeli Neighbourhood Singing National Anthem | Read

PUBLISHED ON: October 11, 2023 | Duration: 1 min, 18 sec

A video of Israelis coming together to show their solidarity in a poignant display of unity and resilience during the ongoing conflict with Gaza has surfaced online. People stepped out onto their balconies, joining a chorus to sing the national anthem of Israel, Hatikva. The State of Israel's official X page (formerly Twitter) shared a video of Israeli neighbourhoods gathering to sing the song on their balconies.
