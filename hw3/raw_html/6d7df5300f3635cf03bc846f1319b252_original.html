It’s forever cold. // Midday Poetry Series. // 097.

PFR

·

Follow

Oct 11

--

It’s forever cold –

The ring on my thigh...

While stargazing

The void sky.



Bruised skin digs

For gold, where

There’s none.



For the wild child

Left on soaked pavements –

Frost is familiar.



Warmth – an alien

Dismissed when

Sun greets Moon.

Thank you for reading!
